Growing E-Commerce

Key Takeaway:

Begin transition to e-commerce retailer

Common E-Commerce Activities

  • Search Engine Optimization
  • Merchandising
  • Digital Community Building
  • Digital Advertising

Search Engine Optimization

Key Tasks:

  • Optimize On-page SEO
  • Image Compression: Speed
  • Process/Maintenance

Notes:

  • Most of this can be done by your team
  • Shared some practices with Abigail
  • De-tangling Search Console
  • Needs to become routine

Merchandising

Key Tasks:

  • Product Descriptions
  • Product Photography
  • Product Recommendations

Notes:

  • Most of this can be done by your team
  • Focus on descriptions in the short term
  • Once you've got more presence, look at AI tools to help with personalization/recommendations. Example: Zaius, Bluecore
  • Streamline photography with tools like Imajize

Digital Community Building

Key Tasks:

  • Post & respond
  • Automated review process
  • Abandoned cart & email remainders
  • Focused (organic) campaigns

Notes:

  • You guys do social really well, keep it up!
  • Product personalization emails
  • Much of this can be automated with good software
  • Organize focused campaigns & grow audience

Digital Advertising

Key Tasks:

  • PPC
  • Google Shopping
  • Display
  • Other channels?

Notes:

  • This will require some expertise
  • Eventually want this done in-house, if possible
  • Expect to lose money before you make it - set aside "risk" capital
  • It needs to be actively managed in-house

Working with an agency

  • Expensive, hard to discern competance, requires management
  • Hire for learning
  • Use your accounts, when possible

Internally

  • Allocate training budget (time, money). Start small (Udemy course)
  • Hire for learning
  • Curious, but quantitative/methodical, too

Ultimately

  • The sky's the limit
  • Will need consistent testing
  • First, a solid foundation, then focus on this
