Why focus?

  • It's the basis for everything else
  • Makes messaging easier
  • Makes finding the right channel easier
  • Makes internal/external alignment easier
  • Increased profitability
  • Makes work more fun
- Everyone wants 'more customers,' but not all customers are created equal. Some are more difficult to work with than others. - We'll see as the basis for everything that the more focused you are, the easier it is to create **profitable** campaigns - Don't spend too much time on how it makes messaging easier
